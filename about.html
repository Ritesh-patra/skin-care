Expert Dermatologist

Dr. Radhika is an accomplished and trusted dermatologist with over 20 years of clinical experience in medical and cosmetic dermatology. She is a distinguished gold medalist known for her precision, knowledge, and commitment to excellence in skin, hair, and aesthetic treatments.
