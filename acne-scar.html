Acne Scar Treatments

Everyone wants to have a bright face, brimming with confidence and self-esteem. However, one glance at the mirror may bring it down if you have pimples, pits, or scars. Scars are trails left behind by pimples. Pimples are mysterious as you never really know when the next one pops up to leave a blemish or pit on your face. Pimples and subsequent scars are a common concern among teens and young adults as most of them meddle with them in front of the mirror.

The causes of pimples are many. In simple words, acne is caused by excessive natural oil (sebum) trapped in the skin pores, causing inflammation that heals with a blemish or pit. Oily skin, hormonal imbalance, and stress can also contribute to acne and scars.

Types of Acne Scars

  • Rolling Scars: Gently sloping edges giving a wavy skin appearance
  • Box Scars: Sharp-edged indentations resembling small boxes
  • Ice-pick Scars: Deep narrow pits looking like puncture wounds
