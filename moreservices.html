    Dermato Surgery: Warts & Moles Removal

    Moles (nevi) and warts are common skin concerns treated safely at our clinic. Moles are clusters of pigmented cells that may appear flat or raised, and while most are harmless, some can be cosmetically unappealing or rarely, problematic. Warts, caused by the human papillomavirus (HPV), are contagious growths that can appear on hands, feet, face, or other areas.

    Our dermatologists use precise surgical and laser techniques to remove moles and warts with minimal discomfort, ensuring safety and aesthetic outcomes. We also evaluate for any atypical moles to prevent potential complications.

    Cosmetic Fillers for Volume Restoration

    As we age, skin loses volume due to natural reduction in collagen, fat, and hyaluronic acid. Dermal fillers are soft tissue injections that restore volume, smooth wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. They are ideal for cheeks, lips, under-eye areas, and nasolabial folds.

    At National Skin Center, we offer temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent fillers, ensuring personalized treatment for natural, youthful results.

    Threadlifts: Non-Surgical Facelift

    Threadlifts provide a minimally invasive solution to sagging skin without surgery. Fine threads are inserted to lift and tighten skin on the face, neck, and jawline.

    This treatment helps reduce jowls, double chin, and sagging cheeks, delivering a smoother and rejuvenated appearance with no visible scars and minimal downtime.

    Double Chin Treatments

    A double chin can form due to age, genetics, or weight gain. Our clinic offers targeted treatments, including fat reduction injections and non-surgical skin tightening, to sculpt the jawline and restore a defined facial profile.

    Stretch Marks Treatment

    Stretch marks result from rapid skin stretching due to growth, weight gain, or pregnancy. Using advanced laser therapy and micro-needling, we reduce the appearance of stretch marks and improve skin texture, making it smoother and more uniform.

    Earlobe Repair & Enhancement

    Torn or elongated earlobes are a common problem from heavy earrings or trauma. Our earlobe repair procedure restores natural shape, minimizes scarring, and allows safe use of earrings again. The treatment is quick, done under local anesthesia, and provides lasting results.

    Derma Roller: Skin Rejuvenation

    Derma rolling, or microneedling, is a popular procedure that stimulates collagen production, reduces fine lines, acne scars, and open pores. The tiny needles create micro-channels that enhance absorption of active serums for better results. Suitable for all skin types, visible improvements are often seen within 4 weeks.

    PRP for Hair Loss

    Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is an innovative treatment for hair thinning and hair loss. Using your own concentrated platelets, growth factors are delivered directly to hair follicles, stimulating regrowth and improving hair density. PRP can be combined with topical or oral treatments for optimal results.

    PRP has a long history in medical science and is a safe, effective, and natural way to rejuvenate hair growth.
